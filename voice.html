OHLOOK is now offering a full-fledged triple threat training program. Enrollment in each of the areas of focus will help equip your young performer with all of the skills necessary to thrive in the competitive world of musical theatre.

Vocal Technique & Performance (8-12)

Vocal Technique & Performance (8-12)

Our young triple threats will develop their voices through practice of proper breathing, posture, tone production, intonation, diction, phrasing and ear training. Students will gain confidence and consistency that will serve as the foundation for their future study of musical theater. They will perform solos and group numbers from popular Broadway shows and other various genres.
