Ticket Info

  • Where are my tickets?
  • Tickets purchased on-line are e-tickets. It is not necessary to print your tickets. Simply check-in at the box office.
  • What time?
  • Doors open half an hour before a show begins. Seating is reserved.
  • Where is it?
  • 1631 W. NORTHWEST HIGHWAY IN GRAPEVINE, unless otherwise specified.
  • Are concessions available?
  • Popcorn, snacks and beverages are available at most shows.
  • Late night shows are BYOB, but we ask that you drink responsibly and we reserve the right to expel anyone who does not abide by that request.
  • What's the "Splatter Zone"?
  • This is only for certain Late Night Shows. Floor seating and the first 2 rows of chairs are considered "splatter zone" seating. This means you will more than likely be covered in blood. Please don't sit here if you are worried about having bloody clothes or are not by nature a fun person!

Tickets purchased online are subject to a service fee. Tickets are also available at the door. Call 817-421-2825 with any questions.

There are no refunds for unused tickets. Tickets may be exchanged for different performance dates if there is availability. For specific requests, please send an e-mail to boxoffice@ohlookperform.com.

Policies and Procedures

Registration & Tuition

  • Your place in class will NOT be confirmed until you have paid the registration fee and 1st month tuition.
  • Masterclass and Production Classes are special classes that are not included in these tuition rates.
  • ALL accounts will be required to have a credit/debit card on file. The card will be charged for tuition on the first of each month. No other form of payment will be accepted for monthly tuition. Any additional charges such as recital fees, costume fees, private lessons, apparel, etc., will have the option to be paid with cash, check, or credit card.
  • Monthly tuition is based on an average of four classes per month. No charge is made when the fifth lesson occurs; however, the studio reserves the right to use these extra classes to offset classes not held due to holidays. The monthly fee remains the same each month regardless of the number of classes in that month.
  • There are no refunds, transfers, credits or extensions for classes not taken, however, absences may be made up in appropriate classes at the same or lower level within six weeks of the missed class.
  • CANCELLATION: If you are going to discontinue any class, you must give us a written cancellation letter, one month prior to canceling the class. Otherwise, you will be charged for the entire month.
  • REGISTRATION FEE: $40 for first child, $20 for each additional child.
