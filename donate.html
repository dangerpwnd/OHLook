How To

To meet the needs of our current enrollment and to continue to serve the North Texas Area with our amazing children’s and community theater program, we are revitalizing and expanding our performing arts center in every way. We are thrilled to be able to improve our beloved home in Grapevine, TX. Big changes; however, require extra help.

Hayden Blalock Scholarship Fund

Hayden Blalock, father of Ohlook founder, Jill Blalock Lord, passed away on November 6, 2018. He loved music and kids, so no surprise that he loved coming to Ohlook and watching the shows. He was always front row, cheering the loudest, taking horrible pictures and supporting all the students.

In his honor we are starting an annual scholarship to be awarded to an Ohlook student that is pursuing musical theater in college. We think he would love this idea and a way to honor his memory for many years to come.
