LATE NIGHT (at ohlook)

LATE NIGHT (at ohlook) is Ohlook Performing Arts Center's premiere stop for late-night comedy theater. Offering up raucous and rarely performed works, LATE NIGHT (at ohlook) is sure to spice up your weekend with a theater experience you won't find elsewhere in the Dallas/Fort Worth metroplex. All shows run Friday and Saturday evenings at 11 PM and are BYOB. Stay turned to this page for all information about upcoming productions.
