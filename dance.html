DRESS CODE

Dancers should be dressed to dance. Tights, leotards, dance shorts and close fitting dance clothes are the only appropriate attire, so that the instructors can observe proper body alignment. Dancers should change into proper dance clothes before class. Hair should be neat and styled to be kept out of the face. NO Jeans, Baggy Clothes, Gum, Food or Drinks other than water allowed in the studio.

  • Combo Classes: Lavender leotard dress (please visit OHLOOK's Discount Dance link) pink tights, hair pulled away from child’s face, pink ballet shoes and black tap shoes.
  • Ballet: Leotard and pink or black tights, hair in a secure bun, pink ballet shoes. Dancers may wear skirts or booty shorts over their leotards.
  • Jazz/Leaps & Turns/Drill Team Prep: Leotard and tights or form fitting tank top or t-shirt, jazz pants, dance shorts, jazz shoes preferably tan.
  • Lyrical/Contemporary: Leotard and tights or form fitting tank top or t-shirt, jazz pants, dance shorts, jazz shoes preferably tan, barefoot or socks.
  • Boys/Men: Form fitting tank top or t-shirt, black jazz pants, black ballet shoes
  • Tap: Hair pulled away from face, comfortable dance clothing, tap shoes.
  • Hip Hop: Clean tennis shoes that are not worn as street shoes, sweatpants or shorts, t-shirt.

You can purchase your dance wear directly from OHLOOK's Discount Dance link

Hip Hop

Lyrical

Lyrical

Lyrical is a fusion of ballet and jazz styles of dance. Driven by the “lyrics” in the music, lyrical is about storytelling through movement and expression. Lyrical dancing is the dance form that inspires movement to express strong emotions, it seeks to connect and entertain through a clear storyline. If you love music with an emotional emphasis this class is for you!

Musical Theater Jazz

Musical Theater Jazz

Jazz is one of the most fun and classic styles of dance! Jazz dancing began with the Jazz music era and is constantly evolving. Jazz is performed on stage with attack, technique and usually with a lot of attitude! In Musical Theater jazz, we’ll drill classic jazz technique and learn fun combinations with a variety of both musical theater and current pop songs. Using its upbeat movement, jazz dance emphasizes rhythm, style, lines, and a strong low center of gravity.
